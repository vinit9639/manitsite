About SAE

SAE is an international organization working towards the growth and development of the automotive industry. The society was established in 1905 with the founding principle to unite scientific and technical staff to perform free academic discussions, to dedicate themselves to the cause of prospering the science and technology for automotive vehicles and to make contributions to speed up the modernization of automotive industry.

About MANIT SAE COLLEGIATE CLUB

The society of automotive engineers at MANIT ventures to promote extensive engineering, designing skills and fabrication dedicated to create innovative solutions and advanced technologies in the field of Automobile.

OBJECTIVE:

  • To design,build, testand market a self designed vehicle.
  • To make students competent to simulate real world design projects in a conducive environment with thorough team spirit and self motivation.
  • To promote healthy network connections by bridging the gap between industries and students for global exposure and quality.

BAJA

MANIT has been a constant participant in BAJA for the past 3 years and has been able to secure remarkable positions. In the very first year of participation, Team Virtuoso made the beginning of MANIT’s Baja journey a very memorable one by securing 11th position. The Legacy of Baja was carried on by Team Dirtborne which was among the TOP 15 teams after the design evaluation. And finally last year again MANITIAN’s proved their mettle by Team Torque finishing in Top 10 of the endurance round and 15th overall.

FUTURE: This year the team will be participating in SAE BAJA OREGON (USA).

SUPRA

We have been a constant participant in supra too and one on the most appreciated performers. With Team INVICTIUS representing MANIT in the inaugural version of SUPRA (2011) in the main event held at MMRT Chennai. Team secured 22nd position among almost 45 participating teams.
