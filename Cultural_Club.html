Function Dramatic and street play

ऐ से ऐनक is the street play society of MANIT-Bhopal. It performs street-plays on social issues at public places, college festivals and inter college events. A streetplay better known as ‘nukad natak’ today, is perhaps the best way to connect with the common man and the youth.These are much intimate, brief, direct and address the social or political message head-on with the help of drama and at the same time educate the audience on a variety of social evils.These plays really stir up emotions, create awareness and lead the way towards a positive change.Some of the topics on which the society has performed are:

  • Eye Donation
  • Deteriorating Higher Education
  • Racism
  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan
  • Child Labor
  • Old age homes

ऐ से ऐनक has been the voice of youth for the past 5 years. It not only delivers performances but propagates its ideas through social media as well. Also, one of their key motive is to propagate the culture of street-play in Bhopal. They are proactive in collaborating with other colleges and performing at key locations in the city

It is truly said that we must not allow other people'slimited perceptions to define us so this is where we step in and tell the world our point of view and make them see what the naked eyes cannot through our ऐ से ऐनक

It has also been the proud participant and winner of a host of inter college competitions held in Bhopal. It also participates in national level competitions like Rendezvous, Cultural Festival of IIT-Delhi and Antaragni, Cultural festival of IIT-Kanpur.

All this is done under the guidance and support of the faculty advisor Dr. Savita Dixit, Professor, MANIT-Bhopal.
